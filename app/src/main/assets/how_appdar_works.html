The privacy promise

Exactly what Appdar does — and doesn't do — with your data.

What it does

Uses your location on-device to find nearby shops
Downloads public shop locations from OpenStreetMap
Saves everything locally, works offline
Lets you remove brands or add your own places

What it never does

Send your location to any server
Collect any personal data
Track your movements or history
Show ads or sell your data
