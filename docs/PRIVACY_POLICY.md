# Privacy Policy – Nearby Apps Widget

**Last updated:** March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Nearby Apps Widget (“the app”) handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## 1. Information We Collect

### Location Data
- **Approximate location:** The app uses Android’s Geofencing API with coarse location (`ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION`) to detect when you are near a business included in our local database (Android 11 and below).
- **Precise location:** On Android 12 and above, the app uses precise location (`ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION`) for the Geofencing API to function reliably.
- **Background location:** On Android 10 and above, the app may request background location permission (`ACCESS_BACKGROUND_LOCATION`) to update the widget when you enter or exit a geofenced area while the app is not in the foreground.
- **How it’s used:** Location data is used solely to determine which business‑app mappings to display. It is processed entirely on your device and is discarded immediately after the widget updates.
- **Sharing:** Your location data is never transmitted to our servers or shared with third parties, unless you explicitly enable the optional geocoding feature (see section 3).

### Local Cache
- **Location history:** To improve widget responsiveness, the app may temporarily cache your recent locations on‑device for up to 24 hours. This cache is never synced to any server and is automatically cleared after 24 hours or when you uninstall the app.
- **Business‑app mappings:** The app maintains a local database of business‑app mappings (business name, package name, geographic coordinates). This data does not contain personal information.

### Sync Data
- **Anonymised information:** If you enable periodic sync, the app may transmit your device’s Android version and locale to our server to ensure compatibility of business‑app mappings. This data is anonymised and cannot be linked to you.

### Optional Geocoding Data
- **Coordinates to address:** If you enable the “Show addresses” feature, the app will send your approximate coordinates to Google’s Geocoding API (a third‑party service) to convert your location into a human‑readable address. This transmission is encrypted and subject to Google’s privacy policy.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To display relevant business apps in the widget based on your current location.
- To improve widget performance (caching recent locations).
- To keep business‑app mappings up‑to‑date (periodic sync).
- To provide optional address display (geocoding).

## 3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. The only circumstance in which your data leaves your device is:

- **Optional geocoding:** If you enable the “Show addresses” feature, your approximate coordinates are sent to Google’s Geocoding API. This is governed by Google’s privacy policy.
- **Anonymised sync data:** Android version and locale may be transmitted to our server for compatibility purposes. This data is anonymised and aggregated.

## 4. Data Retention

- **Location data:** Never stored; discarded immediately after use.
- **Location cache:** Automatically cleared after 24 hours.
- **Business‑app mappings:** Stored locally until you uninstall the app or update the dataset.
- **Sync data:** Retained for 30 days in anonymised form, then deleted.

## 5. Your Controls

- Enable/disable location access in the app’s settings or through Android’s system settings.
- Adjust the detection radius (default 500 m) to control proximity sensitivity.
- Toggle optional features (geocoding, periodic sync) on or off.
- Clear the location cache manually via the app’s settings.
- Uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.

## 6. Security

All data processing occurs on‑device. No personal data is transmitted to our servers except as described above (optional geocoding, anonymised sync). We implement industry‑standard security practices to protect data stored locally on your device.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
