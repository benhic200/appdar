Hey! 👋

I've been building an Android app called Appdar and I'd love you to be one of my beta testers!

Here's what it does:
Appdar is a home screen widget that uses your GPS location to detect when you're near a supermarket, fast food restaurant or coffee shop — and automatically shows the right app on your home screen. So if you walk past a Tesco, the Tesco app appears. Walk past McDonald's, the McDonald's app appears. No searching, no folder diving — it just works.

Currently supports: ASDA, Tesco, Sainsbury's, Morrisons, Lidl, Aldi, McDonald's, KFC, Burger King, Starbucks, Costa, Greggs and many more.

To add you as a tester I'll need the **email address** that's linked to your Google Play Store / Android device. Once I've added you I'll send you a link to download the app for free.

All feedback is welcome — good or bad! If something doesn't work or you think something could be better, let me know.

Thanks so much for helping out! 🙌
